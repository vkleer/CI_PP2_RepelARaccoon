Repel-A-Raccoon!

A game inspired by Whack-A-Mole

Instructions

The goal of the game is to keep as much of your cheese as possible. You start out with 5 cheeses and can get a perfect score by repelling all of the raccoons, keeping all 5 of the cheeses.

  1. Pick a difficulty
  2. Click on a raccoon to repel them - they hate being clicked on!
  3. Try to repel them until the timer reaches 0
  4. Enjoy your cheese!

Story

You returned from your favorite cheese store and bought 5 delicious cheeses to nibble on. What you didn't know is that there is a raccoon who had been lying in wait in your backyard for exactly this moment to strike! He spread the word, and soon all of his furry friends assembled to try and steal your cheese! Can you repel them all and save your dairy dream from disaster?
